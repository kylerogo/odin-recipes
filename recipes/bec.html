Bacon, Egg & Cheese Sandwich

Description

The sandwich you make your family for breakfast, or even lunch and dinner. Use whatever bread and cheese you have in your pantry or fridge.

Many New Yorkers will start their day with a bacon, egg and cheese sandwich - also known as the "hangover cure".

Ingredients

  • ciabatta rolls (or any bread you may have)
  • unsalted butter
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (ow whatever you may have)
